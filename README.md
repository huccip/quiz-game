## About Kwizly

**Kwizly** is a fast-paced, modern trivia quiz app designed to test your knowledge across diverse categories while keeping the experience fun and addictive.

Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore trivia enthusiast, Kwizly offers **endless replayability**, beautiful visuals, and rewarding gameplay mechanics.

### Core Experience
- Massive variety of questions across exciting categories
- Keep your streak alive and watch your score multiply
- Intuitive, buttery-smooth interface

---

## Key Features

### ⚡ Power-Ups
- **Hint** — Get a helpful clue without losing momentum
- **Skip** — Bypass a tough question and protect your streak
- **Time Freeze** — Stop the clock when you need to think
- **Multipliers** (x2, x3, x5) — Supercharge your score on hot streaks
- **Swap** — Replace a difficult question with a fresh one

### 🏆 Achievements & Progression
- Track detailed stats
- Unlock trophies and unique achievements:
  - Quick Thinker
  - Brain Marathoner
  - Casual Cruiser
  - ...and many more
- Challenge yourself with **perfect sessions** (zero mistakes)

### 🌍 Global & Accessible
- **6 Fully Supported Languages**: English, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Arabic
- Full **Light & Dark Mode** support
- Rich **Sound Effects** for every action (clicks, purchases, correct/incorrect answers, etc.)

### ⛓️‍💥 Offline Driven
- No internet access ? **No problem**, the game will not close on you and you can download new quizzes only when necessary

## Tech Stack

- **Language:** Kotlin
- **Architecture:** Modern MVI & Clean Architecture
- **UI:** Jetpack Compose with Material 3
- **Multi-language:** Full localization support
- **Theming:** Dynamic Light & Dark Mode
- **Offline Support:** Takes advantage of different storage options (SharedPrefs, SQLite, ...Etc).
